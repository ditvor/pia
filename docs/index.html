The problem

Context-switching is killing your sprints.

Engineering leads spend 30–40% of sprint planning re-building context that should already be there.

🕵️

Archaeology every Monday

You open a ticket and spend 20 minutes in Slack and git history trying to remember why it exists and what was already tried.

🗺️

Missing codebase map

The ticket says "fix the export bug" but you have to hunt through 40 service files to find the ones that are actually relevant.

📡

Scattered customer signals

Customer urgency lives in Intercom. Usage data is in Amplitude. Neither is visible when you're staring at a YouTrack ticket.

How it works

Three steps. One brief.

💬
Step 1

Type a ticket ID

In your JetBrains IDE chat, type any YouTrack ticket ID. PIA detects it automatically — no commands, no forms.
